About Comp Demand Sim

A Monte Carlo application that translates uncertain assembly demand into component requirements using a bill of materials over a multi-year planning horizon. The default seed data is a PC manufacturing pack; the engine is domain-agnostic.

Core planning question

Given uncertain assembly demand and timing, how many of each component should I expect to need each year, and how variable are those estimates?

Domain model

  • Components — parts whose demand you want to estimate
  • Assemblies — finished goods/configurations with independent demand
  • BOM — components required per assembly unit
  • Demand lines — assembly, quantity, years, distribution

MVP includes

  • Editable components, assemblies, and bill of materials
  • Editable scenario demand lines with fixed / uniform / triangular sampling
  • 1,000–10,000 client-side Monte Carlo iterations
  • Summary stats, CV ranking, percentile planning table, fan chart
  • CSV export and visible independence / scope limitations

Explicitly out of scope (later phases)

  • Inventory, lead times, reorder points, purchase recommendations
  • CSV import and advanced visual analytics
  • Correlation among assemblies, months, and scenarios
  • Multi-level BOMs, multi-user persistence

Stack

  • Angular 22 + TypeScript (client-side simulation)
  • Highcharts fan chart
  • Seeded PRNG for reproducible runs
