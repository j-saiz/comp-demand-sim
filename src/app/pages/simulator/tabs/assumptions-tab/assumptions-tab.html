Model assumptions & limitations

  • Estimates component demand only from uncertain assembly demand (Monte Carlo BOM explosion).
  • Scenario demand lines use the assemblies defined here; totals are spread evenly across planning-horizon years.
  • Does not include inventory, supplier lead times, reorder logic, MOQs, or purchase recommendations.
  • Assumes independent sampling across assemblies and years. This may understate risk when demand moves together.
  • Outputs support planning discussions; combine with operational data before decisions.
