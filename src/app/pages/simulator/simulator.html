Component Demand Simulator

Estimate uncertain component demand from assembly demand and a bill of materials. Seeded with a PC industry pack — the model is assembly-agnostic. For planning conversations only — not procurement or inventory optimization.

Scope: Results estimate component demand from assembly demand uncertainty only. They do not include inventory, lead times, reorder timing, or purchase recommendations.
